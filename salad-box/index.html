Salad Box

Eat more salad

We all know eating vegetables is good for us. But eating veggies is hard. Salad Box makes it easy.

Salad Box is a simple combination of ingredients & instructions that makes eating veggies easy all week long.

Ingredients

1. Red leaf kale

2. Pepper

3. Red cabbage

4. Red onion

Instructions

Do all your prep once and in about an hour you’ll have everything you need for the whole week.

  1. Wash ingredients with fruit & veggie wash
  2. Chop, chop, chop and massage the kale with olive oil
  3. Layer the veggies in the box

Enjoy!

Salad Box keeps your veggies fresh all week. These ingredients are a hearty base for salad, omlettes, soups, sandwiches—whatever your creativity can cook up!
